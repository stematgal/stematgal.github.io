Outdoor STEMinists

We aim to inform and inspire members about STEM-related matters with a focus on environmental impact. We work alongside UC Berkeley's Society of Women Engineers and Bay Area technology companies to give members insight on how companies work.

Our goal is to better inform our members on on their options in STEM and empower them to step forward on matters they are passionate about. The club has an emphasis on being social with other members to form a community. Once a year, members can participate in a club organized camping trip.
