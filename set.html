Science, Engineering, Engineering (SET)

SET provides a space for like-minded people, who are interested in science, engineering, and technology to create and innovate with hands-on projects. While also fostering an environment where everyone can hone their own individual abilities by consistently checking-in and targeting everyones specific talents. Participants can become more competitive in the world of science, engineering, and technology by gaining a deeper knowledge in the different fields and subjects with speakers or informational sessions that SET participants are interested in.

Google Group Sign-Up Form Instagram

Benefits

  • Working on hands-on projects that are in all types of science, technology, and engineering.
  • Bringing new ideas to the table and having those ideas nurtured into creation.
  • Opportunities to be a community advocate.
  • Collaboration and team-work on chosen projects.
  • Increased understanding in many different fields of work and subjects.
  • Good experience to list on college applications.
