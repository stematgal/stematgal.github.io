Galileo Artificial intelligence Society

We strive to educate students who are interested in artificial intelligence and its capabilities. Through collaborative activities, slideshows, and guest speakers, we seek to accommodate eager students with resources that will allow them to latch onto the concepts of artificial intelligence.

Artificial Intelligence is becoming the next greatest thing to take place on Earth due to theversatile and influential applications it has on other STEM disciplines; e.g. cybersecurity. With technology rapidly advancing and its influence exponentially expanding, artificial intelligence will further enhance the practicalities of said technology to propel society into a never before seen direction for the better.

No experience is required to join.
