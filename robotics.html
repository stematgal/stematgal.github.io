Galileo Robotics

We are a student-led team competing in the FIRST® Robotics Competition (FRC) as team FRC4669. The FIRST® Robotics Competition is a world-wide robotics competition with over 3000 teams around the world competing. Learn more at the FIRST® website: firstinspries.org/robotics/frc

Galileo Robotics seeks to provide a fun, engaging, and encouraging environment to consider or have interest in a career of engineering, computer science, or related fields as well as further inspire students to take interest in learning 21st century skills through robotics.

No experience is required to join.

Benefits

  • Compete in the FIRST® Robotics Competition (FRC).
  • Gain experience with hand-tools.
  • Gain skills in machining and manufacturing (CNC milling, 3D Printing, etc.).
  • Solve real-world and hands-on engineering problems.
  • Program a real robot and learn valuable coding skills through real-life application.
  • Build Robots.

Subteams

Our team is divided into 4 main subteams, or groups: Design (CAD), Mechanical, Programming, and Outreach. Each subteam focuses on a different part of robotics. You are not required to stay in one or be in just one. Feel free to try all of them and see which one you prefer best!

No experience is required to join! Please contact the respective subteam lead if you have questions.

Design (CAD) Team

The design team is responsible for the designing of the robot. We 3D model the robot design and its parts. We also manufacture parts using a CNC router and 3D printers.

We use Autodesk Inventor for 3D modeling and parametric design. This is a professional-grade computer-aided design (CAD) program that is used in industry as well as college courses.

CAD Lead: Ethan Quan

More Info

Programming Team

The programming team implements the logic of the robot with code. We program the robot in the C++ language using the framework given by FIRST. In addition to programming, we are also responsible for the entire software side of the robot.

Programming Lead: Melody Liu

More Info

Mechanical Team

The mechanical team is responsible for building the robot and the field. We typically follow the CAD that the Design Team has produced and work together to revise and finalize the design. We also handle prototyping and testing parts that the Design Team cannot. Using hand and power tools, we build the parts to the specifications that the design team provides. We also set up electrical and pneumatics systems in conjunction with the design team.

Additionally, we build the field on which the robot will practice on. This includes any field elements such as the scoring element, climbing element, etc. We build these using the blueprints and specifications that the game manual provides.

Mechanical Leads: Eden Mai, Rina Quan

More Info

Outreach Team

The Outreach Team is responsible for reaching out to potential sponsors for funding, maintaining good relationships between ourselves and our sponsors and the FIRST® community, and managing our team’s social media and Internet presence.

We are responsible for funding our team and representing our team on the internet. We write to companies to request grants to obtain funding for our team. In addition, we also post on Instagram and on some occasions on YouTube.

Outreach Lead: Ian Phan
