Explore the possibilities...

We provide the students of Galileo Academy a multitude of STEM-related extracurricular opportunities and strive to create a comprehensive and well-rounded experience. This coalition seeks match students with the ideal STEM club as well as facilitate cooperation in the Galileo community.

Coalition of STEM Clubs at Galileo

This coalition hopes to facilitate more cooperation between STEM clubs and take advantage of the overlap between clubs by sharing resources and participating in joint projects/events as well as giving members of each club more opportunities to branch out into other clubs and experience their unique offerings.
