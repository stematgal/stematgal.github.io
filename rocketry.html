Galileo Rocketry

We are a student-led team competing in the American Rocketry Challenge (TARC) as team 20-6661. TARC is a rocketry national competition with over 5000 students participating.

We are one of the first high-school level American Rocketry Challenge teams in San Francisco. Learn more at their website: rocketcontest.org

We seek to encourage students to consider aerospace and engineering as a career path as well as give attention to aerospace and space exploration which is often sidelined in school.

No experience is required to join.

Benefits

  • Compete in the American Rocketry Challenge.
  • Learn rocketry and aerospace engineering concepts.
  • Boost for resume and college applications.
  • Gain skills in machining and manufacturing (3D printing, CNC milling, etc.).
  • Build Rockets.
