Galileo Hack Club

Through lectures and hands-on exercises, you will learn programming as well as the principles of networking, cyber-security, penetration testing, and ethical hacking. As you gain experience from demonstrations, you will be able to use your abilities in the real world.

Technology is more accessible than ever before, and with it, criminals desire to harm others. Access to technology and software enables more people than ever before to distribute cyber attacks. Cybercrime will cost the world $10.5 trillion per year by 2025. Only we, as the first generation to be born in the digital age, can combat cybercrime.

Prior experience is not required to join.
